The Cat in the Hack

Kicking the wheels, hitting the ground, we are-a-go! :)

What will you find here?

I often have the luck of picking new and interesting opensource tools to use in my projects. That’s very exciting, challenging, fun!

Then there’s also a cursed side of being an early adopter: when there’s little, stale, or no documentation, no community forums, no articles, talks or blog posts, sample code, or even quora or stack overflow questions around. It a roller-coaster ride!

Recently, my focus has been in the hybrid apps space, using html, css, javascript and typescript, running on node (with loopback), on the desktop (with electron), in the browser and on mobile devices (with angular, ionic, cordova).

When hacking things together, sometimes we find, or come up with, something interesting, beautiful, noteworthy or just plain useful to others.

That’s the kind of thing I’ll be posting about, with opinion tidbits here and there, to add flavor! :)
